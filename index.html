Why ProjectFlow?

Everything your team needs

From task management to file submissions — all in one place designed for academic project workflows.

Team Collaboration

Form teams of 5–6 students, assign a leader, and collaborate seamlessly on shared projects.

Task Management

Create, assign, and track tasks with deadlines. Visual status updates keep everyone in sync.

Progress Tracking

Visual dashboards with charts and progress bars help track milestones and identify blockers.

Teacher Monitoring

Teachers get a bird's-eye view of all teams, with approval flows and feedback tools built in.

Smart Notifications

Real-time alerts for deadlines, teacher feedback, approvals, and task assignments.

File Repository

Upload and manage project files with version history. Submit PDFs, ZIPs, and presentations.
